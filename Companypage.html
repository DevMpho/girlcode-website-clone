Let's Work Together to change the Status Quo!
We need your help to get more young girls and women interested in pursing careers in the Tech space.

Become A Partner

Empower the Future with GirlCoder Clubs

Join us in fostering a passion for technology among young girls! By sponsoring our
GirlCoder Clubs, your company can directly contribute to providing in-person and
online weekend coding classes. Help us inspire and equip girls from grade 4
upwards with essential coding and robotics skills. Be a catalyst for change and
empower the next generation of female technologists!

Shape Careers Through Mentorship

Become a guiding force in the tech industry by sponsoring our Mentorship Program!
Support our 9-week one-on-one initiative that empowers GirlCodes with the
resources they need to navigate and excel in their IT career paths. Your
sponsorship can make a lasting impact on the lives of these aspiring young women,
fostering a diverse and talented workforce for the future.

Host a Career Day and Inspire Change

Showcase your commitment to diversity in the tech sector by sponsoring our
Career Days! Partner with us to host events at your company's offices, exposing our
GirlCoders to successful women in tech careers. Your support will provide
invaluable insights and inspire these young minds to pursue exciting opportunities
in the world of technology.

Fuel Certification Dreams with Online
Bootcamps

Elevate your corporate social responsibility by sponsoring our Online Bootcamp for
Vendor Certification Training! Support our mission to empower girls with a 3-month
intensive program focused on AWS and Microsoft certifications. Your sponsorship
will enable these talented individuals to gain crucial skills, opening doors to
rewarding careers in the tech industry.
